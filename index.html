OASIS HOSTEL

THE BEST HOSTEL IN HIMARA

Located in Himare, Oasis Hostel offers beachfront accommodation 1.1 km from Spille Beach and provides various facilities, such as a garden, a shared lounge and a bar. Located around 1.5 km from Maracit Beach, the hostel with free WiFi is also 2.5 km away from Livadhi Beach. The accommodation offers karaoke and a shared kitchen.

Each room includes a private bathroom with a shower, while selected rooms are fitted with a terrace and others also offer city views. You can play darts at the hostel, and the area is popular for hiking.

The nearest airport is Ioannina, 144 km from Oasis Hostel, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
