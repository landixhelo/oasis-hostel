OUR GALLERY

" Oasis Hostel in Himara offers a vibrant gallery showcasing the essence of local culture
and the beauty of the Albanian Riviera,
creating an inspiring atmosphere for guests to immerse themselves in."

Located in Himare, Oasis Hostel offers beachfront accommodation 1.1 km from Spille Beach and provides various facilities, such as a garden, a shared lounge and a bar. Located around 1.5 km from Maracit Beach, the hostel with free WiFi is also 2.5 km away from Livadhi Beach.
